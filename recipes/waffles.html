Waffles

Description

Waffles are a delicious and easy breakfast to make a relaxing Saturday morning even more perfect. By following this simple recipe you will have hot immaculate waffles in no time at all. This waffle recipe is the only one you'll need to make homemade waffles with you waffle iron. Simple pantry ingredients mix up quickly in this easy batter that can be used right away or stored in the refrigerator for up to a week. Serve waffles hot with whipped cream and fresh fruit or with butter and maple syrup for either breakfast, brunch, or a snack.

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 2
  • Flour: 2 cups
  • Milk: 1 3/4 cups
  • Oil: 1/2 cup
  • Sugar: 1 tbsp
  • Baking Powder: 4 tsp
  • Salt: 1/4 tsp
  • Vanilla: 1/2 tsp
  • Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Whisk eggs in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Add flour, milk, and vegetable oil and mix to combine.
  3. Whisk in sugar, then mix in baking powder, salt, and vanilla just until smooth, being careful not to overmix.
  4. Spray the preheated waffle iron with nonstick spray. Pour batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook until golden brown and the iron stops steaming, 3 to 5 minutes.
